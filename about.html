About Shark Inc. Games

From comic books, to amusing videos, to video games, Shark Inc. as a company has undergone several various platform changes. It was originally established in 2006 as a comic book company, producing titles such as Final Fantasy, Zero Gravity, and even the Survival Journal. It later became Shark Inc. Entertainment and focused on producing comedic short videos and music video parodies. It finally evolved into Shark Inc. Games and focused on the creation and development of various games across multiple platforms. Currently the company is focusing on shifting to web-based game development with WebGL.
